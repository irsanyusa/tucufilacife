What 19 Celebs Would Look Like If Their Faces Fit the Golden Ratio

August 04, 2024

Beauty standards have continued to change over the centuries. But there’s a well-known rule referred to as the Golden Ratio that serves as a form of perfection criteria. We’ve decided to find out what some celebrities would look like if their faces had these ideal proportions. For this purpose, we used the Golden Ratio mask. According to this idea, the ratio of all 3 parts should be absolutely proportional.

Bright Side has chosen 19 celebs to conduct the experiment on, and the results were quite surprising. There’s also a bonus feature at the end where we’ll show you the entire transformation process.
