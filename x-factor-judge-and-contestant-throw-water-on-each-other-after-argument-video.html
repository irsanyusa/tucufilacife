Debbie Stevens exuded confidence when she showed up to audition for The X Factor UK in 2006. She claimed to have the stage presence, the voice, confidence, the looks and everything that it takes to win the show. The judges had completely different opinions about her audition. Check out the audition gone wrong down below.

Debbie Stevens Failed To Impress ‘The X Factor UK’ Judges

Debbie spoke about how she dreamed of standing on a stage with millions of people watching her perform. When she walked into the judging room, Debbie radiated with confidence. “I’m amazing,” she said when asked about her singing talent. For her audition, she sang “When You Believe” by Whitney Houston featuring Mariah Carey. Her rendition failed to impress the judges.

“Your voice is ok, you got no star quality about you. There’s no charisma” Sharon Osbourne said. Debbie was offended that Sharon told her she has no charisma. Simon Cowell agreed with Sharon. He called the performance “a bit pub singerish.” He felt that her strong self-confidence was a bit “deluded.”

“Deborah, the reality is you’re not very good,” Louis Walsh said very bluntly. Viewers could see the anger on Debbie’s face as she received such harsh criticism.

“I really want to slap you now” Debbie warned while pointing at Louis. Check out the compilation below to see 10 contestants who challenged the judges. There have been a bunch of heated exchanges on talent shows that make for some entertaining television moments.

Debbie Threw Water On One Of The Judges After Harsh Critiques

“You are not a good singer” Louis added. Debbie then went on an angry rant directed towards Louis. She has been singing since she was 6 years old and alleges that people tell her how good of a singer she is all of the time.

She told Louis to get out of The X Factor UK audition room. Considering that he is a judge, he did not budge from the judging table. Sharon decided to remove Debbie from the audition room herself. It was clear that she was riled up and wasn’t going to be calming down any time soon.

She took off her jacket and ran back into the audition room. She picked up a cup of water and planned on throwing it at Louis. But Louis knew what was coming and decided to throw a cup of water on her first. Debbie then retaliated and threw a cup of water on Louis. Simon sat in the middle watching all of the drama go down.

Debbie finally left the room for good after announcing that she really wanted to throw one of the glass cups. Simon laughed and told Louis that he can’t just throw water on a contestant whenever he feels like it.
