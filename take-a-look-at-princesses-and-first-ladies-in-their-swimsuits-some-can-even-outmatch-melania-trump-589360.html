Take a Look at Princesses and First Ladies in Their Swimsuits

May 22, 2024
blog

We’ve gotten used to seeing princesses and first ladies in conservative, official clothing that corresponds to their status. But they need some rest too, and like all of us they like to spend their holidays at the beach. The best thing is that they even look excellent wearing a swimsuit and they still manage to hold themselves with royal dignity.

Bright Side would like to show you some photos where the power ladies of the planet can be seen in bikinis and other light clothing.

8. Kate Middleton

MEGA/EAST NEWS, BIGPICTURESPHOTO.COM/EAST NEWS

Kate and William took their vacation in Ibiza in 2006 before they actually married. Tabloids that published this photo mentioned that Kate showed her good taste in choosing her bikini. Its white color underlined her tan, while its shape — her beautiful body.

7. Beatrice of York

Photo by Joel Ryan/Invision/AP /Invision/AP/East News, Fred Duval/MEGA/EAST NEWS

Queen Elizabeth’s granddaughter doesn’t really like official events, and her family supports her in this decision. Instead, Beatrice is focused on her education.

6. Donna Beatrice Borromeo

Marechal Aurore/ABACA/EAST NEWS, D.R./EAST NEWS

Pierre Casiraghi, the younger son of the princess of Monaco, married Beatrice Borromeo, a renowned journalist and aristocrat, in 2015. She was already famous before her marriage and was even the face of Chanel, but then preferred a professional career in journalism.

5. Caroline, Princess of Hanover

Niviere/Villard/SIPA/EAST NEWS, DELLA PORTA/CIAO PIX/East News

Caroline, the eldest child of Rainier III, Prince of Monaco, and the American actress Grace Kelly had to take all her mother’s responsibilities after her tragic death. Caroline also heads the charitable fund of Grace Kelly, and she’s also in charge of the committee of the Festival of Arts.

4. Princess Stéphanie of Monaco

Nebinger Frederic/Blanche Productions/ABACA/EAST NEWS, Eastnews

Princess Stéphanie used to be a singer (a pretty successful one), a fashion designer, and even tried to be an actress. Currently, the princess is the president of several associations, including Monaco Youth Centre and Fight AIDS Monaco. She is also a UN ambassador on HIV/AIDS.

3. Charlene, Princess of Monaco

Charlene was born in Africa and used to be an Olympic swimmer. She got married to Prince Albert II in 2011. Nowadays she’s believed to be one of the most elegant ladies in Europe.

2. Princess Maria-Olympia of Greece and Denmark

Maria-Olympia, the oldest child and only daughter of Pavlos, Crown Prince of Greece and his wife, Marie-Chantal Miller, is not shy when it comes to showing her body in a bikini, even though it contradicts her high status as a princess.

She has profiles on different social networks and shares different photos of her lifestyle with her followers, including her time on the beach.

1. Meghan Markle

Meghan Markle looks great in both conservative clothing and swimwear. Her wonderful looks are due to her healthy diet and regular yoga practice which helps her stay in shape.

Bonus: Queen Elizabeth II
