JoJo Siwa is a celebrity contestant on the current second season of Special Forces: World’s Toughest Test. Siwa recently took to Instagram to share some snippets from the journal she kept during her time on the competition show.

JoJo Siwa Shares Her Special Forces Journal

In her video, Siwa shared that she used the notepad she was given during the show to keep a journal of her experiences. She started reading entries in the journal, starting with the first day, which started at 6:45 a.m.

Siwa even did an impression of Mark “Billy” Billingham’s Australian accent as she narrated one of the tense moments from the season premiere. She also reacted to Billingham saying he didn’t like her, saying it’s now her “mission to make him like me.”

She went on to describe how she and fellow contestant Tom Sandoval got in trouble for filling up their water jugs. Siwa also described the smoked salmon bagel she got to eat on their four-hour drive as “amazing.”

Siwa explained that the contestants didn’t have their phones, but she kept thinking she felt her phone buzzing in her pocket. However, she wrote in her journal that she was “loving every second” of her time on the show.

The Show Airs Monday Nights on FOX

Special Forces: World’s Toughest Test airs Monday nights at 9/8c on FOX. The show premiered last week, with the celebrity contestants also including Jack Osbourne, Tara Reid, Tyler Cameron, Brian Austin Green, Nick Viall, and Blac Chyna.

Siwa told People that she “was psycho” to her fellow contestants Cameron and Viall, saying, “I would stand at the door and be like, ‘Nick, your bottom left button. Tyler, your right shoe. Phone… Not phone. Instead of phone, wallet, keys, you say hat, gloves, armband. Your zipper’s undone. Your button’s undone. Everyone, we’re wearing this, this and that.'”

She said the three of them, along with Bode Miller, “got super close,” adding, “but definitely Nick and Tyler is who I was like, ‘Thank God I have these people here. Because if I didn’t have y’all, nah.'”
