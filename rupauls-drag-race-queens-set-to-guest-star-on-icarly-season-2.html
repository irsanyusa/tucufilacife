In the strangest crossover we didn’t know we needed, multiple iconic queens from RuPaul’s Drag Race are set to guest star during the second season of iCarly. The next season of the Nickelodeon revival will premiere in 2022.

Scarlet Envy, Kandy Muse, Monique Heart, and Rosé are set to “bring some drama” to iCarly this spring. In a promotional video shared to social media, the queens appeared in the classic iCarly loft to “spill the tea honeybee.” If this teaser is any indication, fans can expect to see some fun and fashionable moments when the episodes premiere.

“This was so f*cking fun I can’t wait for y’all to see it,” Rosé Tweeted.

There is no news yet as to how many episodes will feature the queens, but the show is sure to bring all the RuPaul drama that we know and love. As many fans have pointed out, this is the best of our past T.V. obsession combined with our present, and we’re totally here for it.

iCarly‘s Paramount+ Revival

iCarly returned to bless our 2021 with the nostalgic sitcom we all know and love. The revival stars Miranda Cosgrove, Jerry Trainor, and Nathan Kress in their original roles along with Laci Mosley and Jaidyn Triplett as new characters.

The new series takes place nine years after the previous show ended. Carly has moved back to Seattle, and her loveable good of an older brother, Spencer, is now a wealthy artist after accidentally creating a renowned work of art.

Sadly, our dear Freddie is twice-divorced with a failed tech start-up, and is once again living with his mother back in the same apartment building where the group grew up. Carly, Freddie, and Spencer have relaunched the iCarly web show, so we can only hope the RuPaul queens are coming to the loft to bring some flare to the internet.

You can catch iCarly and RuPaul’s Drag Race Season 14 this spring exclusively on Paramount+.
