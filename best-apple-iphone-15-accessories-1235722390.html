The iPhone 15 Doesnt Come With a Charger or Earbuds: Heres How to Get These Accessories

July 17, 2024
blog

Since 2020, Apple has left out a wall charger and earbuds in the iPhone's packaging. If you don't have any laying around at home, you can pick some up here.

If you purchase an independently reviewed product or service through a link on our website, Variety may receive an affiliate commission.

Starting with the iPhone 12 release in September 2020, Apple began shipping its smartphone models without a wall charger and earbuds, citing environmental concerns from excess packaging. While most people eventually bought these accessories to pair with their phones, you may have to pick up a few additional items now, with the unveiling of Apple’s new iPhone 15.

The iPhone 15 — and its new USB-C port for charging and data transfer — means many iPhone users will need a new wall charger to power up their phone. The iPhone 15 also once again does not come with earbuds — or even a slot to plug in wired earbuds — so you’ll have to find a workaround for that as well.
