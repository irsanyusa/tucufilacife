Six-Year-Old TikTok Star Zoe Erianna to Audition for 'America's Got Talent'

America’s Got Talent season 18 is about to witness how a six-year-old from Drexel Hill can change the world through music. Singer-songwriter Zoe Erianna will perform in front of the AGT crowd to show off her powerful vocal skills.

Zoe Erianna is a TikTok Singing Sensation

When the pandemic gave rise to years-long of lockdown, three-year-old Zoe Erianna Cui taped singing videos for her church’s online service. These videos have eventually made its way to the internet, then receiving intense praises from the public. Zoe was able to attract thousands of followers on both Instagram and TikTok where her parents primarily upload her music-related content.

“Zoe Erianna has only been on planet Earth for a short 6 years, but she sounds like she has a whole lifetime of experience,” her website bio reads.

According to her website, Zoe’s inclination towards music and entertainment were first discovered when she was a baby. Her parents found out that she has a perfect pitch when she was two years old. Regarded as a “music prodigy,” Zoe can already write and compose her own songs.

As a young kid, Zoe has expressed her desire be on stage. It’s thus no surprising that Zoe is game in competing in a huge reality talent show like AGT.

She’s Appearing on America’s Got Talent Season 18

In an attempt to showcase her talent beyond TikTok, Zoe joins America’s Got Talent and is bound to impress the show’s judging panel. On Instagram, Zoe’s mother Arlene announced that their entire family went to California to accompany the six-year-old to her audition.

“A few weeks ago we flew out to Cali for Zoë’s audition!” her mother wrote. “Only 2 weeks away until the biggest reality show on TV airs with the biggest stage in the world!!!”

Arlene posted the announced alongside a series of their travel snaps. Evidently, the Cui family is excited for Zoe and her journey on AGT.

America’s Got Talent began its season 18 with a two-hour premiere that televised a shadow puppeteer and a dancer in an inflatable dinosaur costume. It has also aired the Mzansi Youth Choir which has obtained the first ever, audience Golden Buzzer. Fans are eager to see this week’s new episode, airing Tuesday, June 6 at 8 p.m. EST.
