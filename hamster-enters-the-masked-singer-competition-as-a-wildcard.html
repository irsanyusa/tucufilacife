We’ve been waiting for this character since The Masked Singer began sharing their new costumes for the season. This character was first hinted in the promotional video for the sixth season. The hamster looked even better than they originally did in the promotional video, who could they be?

The Hamster walked on stage in total glory, they knew they would own it. They took to the stage singing “Pretty Woman” by Roy Orbison. This masked performer showed off a deep voice and some killer dance moves.

At the end of their performance they hugged Nick Cannon. Then peed on Cannon a little…which could be a huge clue for the panelists.

“You’re entertaining, but most importantly, you can sing!” said Jenny McCarthy.

During the original set of clues for this wildcard they were described as “stuck in a wheel.” They are considered to be a person who jumps around from one project to the next and is in all kinds of arenas.

They also talked about being from a place with lots of sun and warmth alongside a picture of Alcatraz Island. This clue automatically made panelists guess that they’re from San Francisco, CA.

For the second set of clues, the Men in Black brought a locker on stage. Since it is back to school night, the clues were delivered in a locker. The locker had baseball gear in it and was said to be really stinky since the character is from somewhere warm.

America’s Initial Guesses

Based off of the clues and performance, the panelists guessed comedians like Billy Murray, Albert Brooks, Jack Black, Tim Allen, and more. The guesses for this mask were all acting roles, which is almost entirely opposite of America’s Initial guesses.

When this reveal was first shared fans immediately noticed the character wearing a set of bedazzled headphones and a giant multi-colored necklace. They thought this character would be either a rapper or a DJ. I wonder if this wildcard character will actually be a DJ or if one of the panelists guessed right!
